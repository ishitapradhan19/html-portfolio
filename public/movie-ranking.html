The Best Movies According to Ishita

My top 3 movies of all-time.

A silent voice

This is my favorite anime movie. It tackles strong themes like anxiety, depression, and bullying and teaches us to be kind to each other.

Your Name

This is my favorite romance anime. I love both the animation and plot in this movie.

Dune 2

The best movie I watched this year. A must watch for sci-fi and action lovers.
